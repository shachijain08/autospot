Our Mission

Our company started with a dream. 4 kids with no experience, only a firey passion to change the world. Autospot is the spark that ignited that flame. A comprehensive network-based live service that provides much needed insight into the vehicles that we all use but hardly know anything about, Autospot aims to be a platform that provides a saftey net for any user stuck in a tight spot without the expertise they might need. Curated by a passionate team of researchers and developers, the trajectory for autospot is nowhere but up. We hope that you join us on our journey and help us transform the world for the better.
